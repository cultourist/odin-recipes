Description

Ingredients (for 2 people):

  • 250g curd cheese
  • 150g flour
  • 1 egg
  • 250g strawberries/plums/apricots

Steps:

  1. Mix the curd cheese and flour with the egg. Add some salt.
  2. Form with the dough small balls with one fruit in the middle.
  3. Put them in a pot with boiling water and reduce the heat.
  4. After around 10-15 minutes the dumplings are ready and should swim on top.
