Curd Cheese Dumplings (Topfenknödel)

Home

Description

Ingredients (for 2 people):

  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 250g cheese (e.g. Gouda)

For the meat filling:

  • 500g of minced meat
  • 500ml of tomato sauce
  • 1 spoon of tomato purée
  • 1 Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For the bechamel:

  • 150ml milk
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • A handful of cheese (e.g. Gouda)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut the onion into small pieces and fry it in oil in a pot
  2. Add the meat to the onion and fry it until it is well-done
  3. Add the tomato purée and mix everything
  4. Add the tomato sauce and reduce the heat after it turned hot. It shouldn't cook.
  5. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  6. For the bechamel we need to melt the butter in another pot
  7. Add the flour and mix everything. Watch that nothing gets burned
  8. Add the milk and a handful of cheese and mix everything. Reduce heat. It should be hot but not cooking
  9. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  10. Prepare a casserole and coat it with butter
  11. Add a thin layer of the tomato meat sauce
  12. Put one layer of lasagna noodles on it.
  13. Now there is again one layer of tomato meat sauce followed by a layer of bechamel, cheese and then again lasagan noodles
  14. The last layer should be one of lasagna noodles with cheese on top
  15. Put the lasagna in the oven for 30 minutes at 200 degrees Celsius.
