Description

Ingredients (for 4 people):

  • 250g Chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 250g cream

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate with steam in a pot.
  2. Separate the yolk from the egg white.
  3. Mix the egg white to beaten egg whites.
  4. Mix the yolk.
  5. Merge the mixed yolk with the beaten egg whites and add the melted chocolate. Afterwards mix everything.
  6. Pour the still fluid mass in a bowl and put in a fridge. After 3 hours it's ready to eat.
